Tech for a smarter Buffalo a more equitable Buffalo an open Buffalo a healthier Buffalo

Smart Buffalo Collaborative is a civic organization and newly-formed local brigade of Code for America. We are a diverse and inclusive group of civic technologists, citizen activists, data geeks, policy wonks, public servants, and curious residents.

We share the common goal of improving access to digital skills, promoting open government, and harnessing technology expertise to enhance public services and support communities in building a stronger, smarter, and more equitable Buffalo.

OUR PILLARS

COLLABORATION.

The Smart Buffalo Collaborative team shares a passion for analysis and informed collaboration. We build new, creative tools with communities and cultivate a network of expert volunteers dedicated to community service and civic engagement.

EQUITY.

We support digital skills training programs and push for equitable access to internet and digital resources for City of Buffalo residents.

OPENNESS.

We are guided by transparent procedures and build an open, participatory environment for all members. We use open source code.
